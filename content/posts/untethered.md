Having left my previous job, not having decided my next job, I'm exploring the remaining Smithsonian museums, completing the sense untethered.

Last time I was exploring the Smithsonian museums, I was checking Slack and had to interrupt my exploration to handle issues as the only engineer on my project. This time I have no deadline except when the museums close. If I don't see something today, I can see it tomorrow; if I don't see something tomorrow, I can see it whatever day.

To top off the mood, I'm listening to ethereal albums. In ["Atemporal" from _¡Ay!_ by Lucrecia Dalt](https://genius.com/Lucrecia-dalt-atemporal-lyrics):
> Me reconozco en esa roca atemporal\
> ...\
> Lágrimas de xenocristales\
> De circones hadeanos\
> Redistribuyendo el mismo\
> El mismo tiempo

Translated:
> I recognize myself in that timeless rock\
> ...\
> Tears of xenocrystals\
> Of [Hadean zircons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hadean_zircon)\
> Redistributing the very same\
> The very same time

Music for the sense, the mood untethered. See also [the soundtrack for Marty Supreme by Daniel Lopatin](https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/daniel-lopatin-marty-supreme-original-soundtrack), and [_Tranquilizer_ by Lopatin as Oneohtrix Point Never](https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/oneohtrix-point-never-tranquilizer).
