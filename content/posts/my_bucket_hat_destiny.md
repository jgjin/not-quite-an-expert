Before I travel, I should get a hat to protect my face and neck from the sun.

Since I managed to lose [2 Uniqlo baseball caps](https://www.typicalcontents.com/articles/the-best-baseball-caps), this time I'll try procuring a bucket hat, which should also protect my neck from the sun.

When I tried [the Uniqlo bucket hat](https://www.uniqlo.com/us/en/products/E482816-000) at the store, I saw a middle-aged Asian dad in the mirror 🤔🪣. I could imagine myself in incredible detail squinting under the sunlight over one of [the Wonders of the World](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wonders_of_the_World), discovering the camera button on the iPad is exactly where it was last time.

Well, as I see it, I could either look middle-aged through my clothes with the bucket hat on, or look middle-aged through my skin with the bucket hat off. I'm going to look middle-aged eventually anyway, so I'll keep it and embrace my bucket hat destiny.

Update: I managed to lose the Uniqlo bucket hat since publishing this post. Maybe I'll get another one.
